Online Submission Guideline
Thank you for considering IOASR for your manuscript. Please follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth submission process.
  1. General Submission Instructions
    • Account Registration: If you do not already have an account, you will need to register by providing your name, email address, and affiliation.
    • Submission Steps:
      • Log in to the submission system.
      • Follow the step-by-step instructions to upload your manuscript.
      • Complete all required fields in the submission form, including the title, author information, keywords, and abstract.
  2. Manuscript Preparation
    • File Format: Submit your manuscript as a Microsoft Word document (.doc or .docx). PDF submissions are not accepted.
    • Length and Formatting:
      • Ensure the manuscript follows the journal's style guidelines, including structure, and APA references style.
      • Use single spacing, a standard font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12 pt), and 1-inch margins.
  3. Additional Required Documents
    • Figures and Tables: Include Figures and Tables embedded within the manuscript. If they are not embedded, upload figures, tables, and other supporting files separately. Each file should be clearly labeled (e.g., Figure1.jpg, Table2.doc).
    • Supplemental Materials: If your submission includes supplemental materials (e.g., data sets, additional figures), upload these as separate files and indicate in your cover letter that they should be published as supplementary content.
  4. Review and Response Time
    • Acknowledgment of Receipt: You will receive an automatic email acknowledgment after submitting your manuscript.
    • Tracking Your Submission: You can track the status of your manuscript by logging in to the submission system.
    • Review Timeline: The review process typically takes 4-6 weeks. You will be notified once a decision has been made.
Author Checklist
Before submitting, please ensure that:
  • The manuscript and all supplementary files are ready for submission.
  • The manuscript adheres to our formatting and citation guidelines.
  • All co-authors have reviewed the final version and approved the submission.

Email Submission Guidelines
Thank you for considering submitting your work to IOASR. Please carefully follow the guidelines below when preparing your manuscript for submission via email.
  1. General Submission Instructions
    • Email Address: Send your manuscript to submission@ioasr.org
    • Subject Line: Include “Manuscript Submission” followed by the name of the selected journal.
    • Body of the Email: Please include the following information:
      • Title of the manuscript
      • Full name(s) of the author(s) with affiliations
      • Corresponding author’s contact information (email, phone number, and country)
      • Type of Article (Original Research Article, Review Article, Case Report, etc.)
  2. Manuscript Format
    • File Format: Manuscripts should be submitted as a Microsoft Word document (.doc or .docx), not PDF (.pdf).
    • Length and Formatting:
      • Ensure that the manuscript adheres to the journal’s style guide, including any word limits, formatting, and referencing style.
      • Use single spacing, a standard font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12 pt), and 1-inch margins.
  3. Additional Required Documents
    • Cover Letter: Attach a cover letter as a separate document. The letter should include:
      • A brief introduction of the manuscript
      • A statement confirming that the work is original and not under review elsewhere
      • Any potential conflicts of interest
    • Figures, Tables, and Supplemental Files: Include Figures and Tables embedded within the manuscript. If they are not embedded, send these as separate files. Each file should be clearly labeled (e.g., Figure1.jpg, Table2.doc).
