Refund Policy
Refund Eligibility:
Authors who have made payments for journal publication or individual publisher services through himjournals.com, under Iarcon, may be eligible for a refund under certain circumstances.
Refunds will only be considered for valid reasons such as technical errors, double payments, or fraudulent transactions.

Refund Process:
Authors must submit a refund request to himjournals.com, detailing the reason for the refund and providing any relevant evidence to support their claim.

Upon receiving a refund request, himjournals.com will conduct a thorough investigation to verify the validity of the claim and assess the evidence provided.

If the claim is deemed valid, himjournals.com will process the refund within a reasonable timeframe.

Exclusions:
Refunds will not be provided for delays in publication or any other publication-related queries.

himjournals.com reserves the right to reject refund requests that do not meet the eligibility criteria or lack sufficient evidence to support the claim.

Dispute Resolution: In cases where there is a dispute regarding a refund request, himjournals.com will engage in a fair and impartial resolution process.

Both the author and himjournals.com will have the opportunity to present their arguments and provide evidence to support their respective positions.

himjournals.com will make a decision based on the evidence presented and will communicate the outcome to the author in a timely manner.

Legal Action:
If a refund request involves fraudulent activity or a violation of the terms and conditions, himjournals.com reserves the right to take legal action against the responsible parties. himjournals.com will cooperate with law enforcement authorities and provide any necessary evidence to support legal proceedings.

Policy Revision:
This refund policy may be revised or updated periodically to ensure its effectiveness and compliance with relevant laws and regulations.
Any changes to the refund policy will be communicated to authors through the himjournals.com website or other appropriate channels.
